(mu,n,sigma) = (10348, 100, 2500)
# Part a
mean = mu
std_error = sigma/np.sqrt(n)

# Part b
vary = 200
(value_1,value_2) = (mu-vary, mu+vary)
(z_1,z_2) = ((value_1-mu)/std_error,(value_2-mu)/std_error)
print('The probability the sample mean will be within plus/minus 200 of the population',
      f'mean is {stats.norm.cdf(z_2) - stats.norm.cdf(z_1)}')
# Part c
threshold = 12000
z_threshold = (threshold - mu)/std_error
print(f'The probability the sample mean will be greater than 12000 is {1-stats.norm.cdf(z_threshold)}') 

The probability the sample mean will be within plus/minus 200 of the population mean is 0.5762892028332067
The probability the sample mean will be greater than 12000 is 1.9477308654813896e-11
